An Interactive Explorer for Yelp

Created by Sriharsha Kanumilli

Interact with the widgets on the left and enter your food preference and location. Up to 50 restaurants will be displayed. Hover over the dot to learn more.

A credibility metric was introduced. This makes sure that business with 1000 reviews are favored more than businesses with 20 reviews, allowing you to compare restaurants by their popularity as well as rating.

The warmer the circle color, the higher the business's credibility score. The larger the circle, the pricier the business.
